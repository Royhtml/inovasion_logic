Professional Espresso Blend

Composition: 70% Brazil, 30% Sumatra

Roast Level: Dark

Tasting Notes: Dark Chocolate, Caramel, Full-bodied

Best for: Espresso, Cappuccino, Latte

Recommended Brewing: 18g dose, 25-30 second extraction, 36g yield

Our signature espresso blend is crafted specifically for professional use. The Brazilian beans provide sweetness and body, while the Sumatran component adds depth and complexity. This blend produces excellent crema and works well with milk.

Art of Coffee

The Barista's Craft

1. Premium Bean Selection

Our baristas meticulously select specialty-grade Arabica beans from sustainable farms, ensuring only the top 2% of harvests make it to your cup.

2. Artisan Roasting

Each batch is small-roasted to perfection using time-honored techniques, developing complex flavors while preserving the bean's natural characteristics.

3. Expert Brewing

Our certified baristas use precision equipment and exacting standards to extract the perfect cup, balancing temperature, time, and technique.

The Barista Difference

Every cup tells a story - from the high-altitude farms where our beans are grown to the skilled hands that craft your beverage. Our baristas complete over 100 hours of training in extraction science, milk texturing, and flavor profiling to ensure each drink meets our exacting standards of excellence.
